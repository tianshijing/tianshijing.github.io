Juanxi Tian received an Honours Bachelor of Science degree in Artificial Intelligence from Hong Kong Baptist University in 2025. He is currently serving as a research assistant at the LLM Research Center jointly affiliated with The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, and the Shenzhen Big Data Research Institute. During his undergraduate studies, he began collaborating with several universities and research institutions, including Westlake University, Peking University, the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), and Alibaba. His research primarily focuses on Efficient AI, Vision Foundation Models, and MLLMs, with the goal of exploring efficient and scalable, large-scale vision-centric multimodal foundation models. He has previously published papers at top-tier conferences such as NeurIPS, CVPR, and ACL, and has served as a reviewer for conferences and journals including ICML, ICLR, NeurIPS, CVPR, ECCV, ARR, and TNNLS.
