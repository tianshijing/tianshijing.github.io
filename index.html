Juanxi Tian is an incoming PhD student at the Nanyang Technological University, Physical Vision Group (PVG), supervised by Dr. Chuanxia Zheng. Currently, he serves as a research intern at the Shanghai AI Lab, and will graduate with a Bachelor's degree in Artificial Intelligence from Hong Kong Baptist University in 2025.

During his undergraduate studies, he has collaborated with several prestigious universities and research institutions, including Westlake University, Peking University, UIUC, OPPO, and Alibaba.

His research primarily focuses on Generative AI and Foundation Models, with the goal of exploring efficient and scalable, large-scale (vision-centric) multimodal foundation models towards world models.

He has published papers at top-tier conferences such as NeurIPS, CVPR, and ACL, and has served as a reviewer for leading conferences and journals including ICML, ICLR, NeurIPS, CVPR, ECCV, ARR, and TNNLS.
