Juanxi Tian is an incoming PhD student at the Nanyang Technological University, Physical Vision Group (PVG), supervised by Dr. Chuanxia Zheng. Currently serving as a research intern at the Shanghai AI Lab, he graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Artificial Intelligence from Hong Kong Baptist University in 2025.

His research primarily focuses on Generative AI and Efficient AI （Optimization）, with the goal of exploring Efficient and Scalable, Large-Scale (Vision-Centric) Multimodal Foundation Models → World Models. He has published papers at top-tier conferences such as NeurIPS, CVPR, and ACL, and serves as a reviewer for leading conferences and journals.
