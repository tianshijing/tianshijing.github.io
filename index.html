About

My research focuses on (M)LLM and optimization, particularly in understanding the complex phenomena of deep learning systems. I aim to develop more robust and general techniques through empirical analysis and optimization. I enjoy exploring various scenarios to conduct interesting analyses and improvements, even if it involves a lot of trial and error. This process has taught me a great deal. I plan to share meaningful insights and findings through a blog in the future. Stay tuned for updates!
