Research Overview

Juanxi Tian's Research Journey

Exploring the frontiers of Generative AI and Foundation Models to build intelligent systems that understand and interact with the world.

Research Vision

My research focuses on developing visual intelligence to understand the physical world, building upon video understanding as a core perceptual mechanism. I aim to create efficient and scalable, large-scale (vision-centric) multimodal foundation models that can serve as stepping stones toward world models.

With the belief that "pretraining as we know it will end," I explore how the future of artificial intelligence lies in aligning with human intelligence—and ultimately surpassing it through innovative architectural designs and optimization strategies.

Research Interests

Current focus areas and ongoing investigations

Foundation Models

Investigating architectural design and coupling bias in large-scale vision/language models. Developed BOCB analysis and SGG optimization framework for scalable training under resource constraints.

BOCB Paper • SGG Paper
Efficient & Scalable AI

Developing efficient methodologies for large model training including speculative decoding, advanced optimizers, and domain-specific optimization techniques that remain effective as model size continues to grow.

Optimization Scalability
Unified Generation & Understanding

Building bridges between visual representation learning and generative modeling through shared frameworks. Exploring architectural design, data composition, and training paradigms toward AGI.
